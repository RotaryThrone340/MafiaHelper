# Mafia Helper

A tool built to help **Mafia narrators** keep track of roles, kills, and everything in between — so you can focus on running the game instead of remembering who did what.

## What It Does

- Guides the narrator step by step through each night and day phase
- Tracks who is alive, who was killed, and who was saved
- Supports physical card games and cardless play
- Handles role assignment automatically or manually

## Who It's For

- Narrators who want a smoother, more organized game
- Anyone who loves Mafia but doesn't have a deck of cards handy
